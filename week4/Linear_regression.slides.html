Linear Regression¶

Linear regression is a statistical approach that models the relationship between input features and output.

The input features are called the independent variables, and the output is called a dependent variable.

Our goal here is to predict the value of the output based on the input features by multiplying it with its optimal coefficients.

Some real-life examples of linear regression :

(1) To predict sales of products.

(2) To predict economic growth.

(3) To predict petroleum prices.

(4) To predict the emission of a new car.

(5) Impact of GPA on college admissions.
